Menu

Mains

  • Pork Belly Adobo Bowl

    -Slow-braised pork belly in our classic soy-vinegar adobo sauce, served over steamed rice.

  • Pork BBQ Skewers + Rice

    -Juicy marinated pork grilled in our sweet & smokey Filipino style glaze, served over fried rice and pickled papaya on the side.

  • Pansit Bihon

    -Thin rice noodles stir-fried with garlic and veggies, with a choice of 1 BBQ skewer or 2 pieces of lumpia.

Snacks

  • Lumpia Klassik

    3 pieces of crispy Filipino spring rolls filled with seasoned pork and veggies.

  • Firecracker Lumpia

    3 pieces of crispy lumpia packed with cheesy jalapeno goodness for a spicy punch.

  • Supreme Lumpia

    3 pieces of crispy lumpia topped with cheese sauce, sour cream, honey glazed bacon, and green onions.
